Ember's Unofficial F-Droid Repo

This repository allows F-Droid users to install Freedoom, uGet and Signal on their device without going through Google Play. These apks are sourced pre-compiled from the respective app developers and are not compiled by the repo maintainer.

To begin, scan the QR code above or enter the following URL into F-Droid:

https://fdroid.heartshine.xyz/repo

Software offered:

  • Freedoom: A GZDoom port to Android with on-screen touch controls. (GitHub)
  • uGet: a powerful multi-threaded download manager. (Homepage, Sourceforge)
  • Signal: encrypted chat, video and audio calling, group messages and more for the privacy-conscious. (Homepage, GitHub)

**I have noticed increased traffic coming from Tor endpoints lately, and have adjusted my Cloudflare firewall to accommodate. Tor users should no longer be blocked from this repo.
